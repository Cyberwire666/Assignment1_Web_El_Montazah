About El Montaza

The Montazah Gardens Complex, which is located on the eastern edge of Alexandria on the Al-Montazah Bay, covers an area of 61 hectares.
This extensive park surrounds Arab palaces as well as luxury hotels. On its grounds there are sandy city beaches that stretch along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea.
